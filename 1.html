Earthquake

Causes: Tectonic plate movement, volcanic activity.

Safety Measures: Drop, Cover, Hold, move to open areas.

Flood

Causes: Heavy rainfall, dam failures, storm surges.

Safety Measures: Move to higher ground, avoid floodwaters.

Forest Fire

Causes: Dry weather, lightning, human negligence.

Safety Measures: Evacuate, stay low, use wet cloth over mouth.

Landslide

Causes: Heavy rain, earthquakes, deforestation.

Safety Measures: Avoid slopes, move away from landslide paths.
